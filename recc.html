Reccomended

I generally tend to like games with some sort of open worldness

  • Star Wars: The Old Republic
    Good MMORPG to play, especially if you’re into Star Wars.
  • Garry’s Mod
    If you want an extremely easy to mod sandbox game, this is for you, big following and many different servers for many different ways to play.
  • Terraria
    A classic that brings out the builder in you.
  • Skyrim
    “It just works” - Todd Howard.
  • Reassembly
    Build drones, fight fleets with players and play in a massive singleplayer map.
